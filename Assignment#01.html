  • Water

  • Sauce (e.g., marinara, Alfredo, pesto, etc.)
  • Vegetables (optional, e.g., tomatoes, mushrooms, spinach, bell peppers, etc.)

STEPS FOR MAKING PASTA

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  4. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a separate pan over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic and chopped onion to the pan and sauté until golden brown.
  6. Pour tomato sauce into the pan with the garlic and onion mixture.
  7. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Simmer the sauce for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the pan with the sauce.
  10. Toss the pasta with the sauce until evenly coated.
  11. Serve the pasta dish hot, optionally topping it with grated Parmesan cheese.

About Pasta

Pasta is a type of Italian cuisine made from unleavened dough of wheat flour, water, and sometimes eggs.

My Favorite Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Combine cooked pasta and sauce.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and Parmesan cheese.
