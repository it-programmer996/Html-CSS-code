My Favorite Dish - Pasta

About Pasta

Pasta is a type of Italian cuisine made from unleavened dough of wheat flour, water, and sometimes eggs.

Ingredients for Pasta

  • Pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, fettuccine, etc.)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Sauce (e.g., marinara, Alfredo, pesto)
  • Vegetables (optional)
  • Herbs and spices
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps for Making Pasta

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add salt and pasta.
  3. Cook until al dente.
  4. Prepare sauce in a pan.
  5. Mix pasta with sauce.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
